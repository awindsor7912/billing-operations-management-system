<section class="section" id="overview">
  <div class="container">
    <div class="section-header">
      <h2>Project Overview</h2>
      <p>
        The Billing Operations Management System (BOMS) was designed to address inefficiencies in
        billing workflows caused by manual tracking, inconsistent process execution, and limited
        visibility into operational status. The system introduces structured workflow automation and
        centralized tracking to support more reliable billing operations.
      </p>
    </div>

    <div class="card-grid">
      <article class="card">
        <div class="card-meta">
          <span class="tag">Business Problem</span>
        </div>
        <h3>Manual and Fragmented Billing Processes</h3>
        <p>
          Billing workflows were dependent on manual tracking, ad hoc communication, and inconsistent
          process execution, leading to inefficiencies and increased risk of missed deadlines or errors.
        </p>
        <ul>
          <li>Manual filtering, tracking, and status updates across workflows.</li>
          <li>Limited visibility into task ownership and completion status.</li>
          <li>Higher risk of delays, rework, and compliance gaps.</li>
        </ul>
      </article>

      <article class="card">
        <div class="card-meta">
          <span class="tag">Solution</span>
        </div>
        <h3>Structured Workflow Automation System</h3>
        <p>
          BOMS introduces a centralized system using SharePoint and Power Automate to standardize
          workflows, automate key processes, and provide real-time visibility into billing operations.
        </p>
        <ul>
          <li>Centralized tracking of billing workflows and task status.</li>
          <li>Automated process steps to reduce manual intervention.</li>
          <li>Improved visibility into deadlines, ownership, and workflow progress.</li>
        </ul>
      </article>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>

<section class="section" id="approach">
  <div class="container">
    <div class="section-header">
      <h2>Approach</h2>
      <p>
        The system was designed with a focus on process standardization, automation, and operational
        visibility, ensuring that workflows are both repeatable and easier to manage at scale.
      </p>
    </div>

    <div class="card-grid">
      <article class="card">
        <div class="card-meta">
          <span class="tag">Workflow Design</span>
          <span class="tag">Automation</span>
        </div>
        <h3>Process Standardization</h3>
        <p>
          Billing workflows were restructured into defined, repeatable processes supported by automated
          actions and consistent tracking logic.
        </p>
        <ul>
          <li>Defined workflow stages to reduce ambiguity in process execution.</li>
          <li>Standardized handling of billing tasks across the team.</li>
          <li>Reduced variability caused by manual decision-making.</li>
        </ul>
      </article>

      <article class="card">
        <div class="card-meta">
          <span class="tag">Power Platform</span>
          <span class="tag">Automation</span>
        </div>
        <h3>Automation of Manual Tasks</h3>
        <p>
          Power Automate was used to eliminate repetitive administrative steps, allowing the team to
          focus on validation and exception handling rather than routine processing.
        </p>
        <ul>
          <li>Automated filtering, notifications, and task progression.</li>
          <li>Reduced reliance on manual spreadsheet manipulation.</li>
          <li>Improved consistency in process execution.</li>
        </ul>
      </article>

      <article class="card">
        <div class="card-meta">
          <span class="tag">Visibility</span>
          <span class="tag">Tracking</span>
        </div>
        <h3>Centralized Workflow Visibility</h3>
        <p>
          A centralized SharePoint structure provides clear visibility into workflow status, ownership,
          and deadlines across billing operations.
        </p>
        <ul>
          <li>Real-time view of task status and progression.</li>
          <li>Clear ownership assignment for accountability.</li>
          <li>Improved coordination across team members.</li>
        </ul>
      </article>

      <article class="card">
        <div class="card-meta">
          <span class="tag">Controls</span>
          <span class="tag">Compliance</span>
        </div>
        <h3>Process Control and Compliance</h3>
        <p>
          The system supports better process control by making workflow steps visible and easier to
          validate, reducing the likelihood of missed actions or incomplete processes.
        </p>
        <ul>
          <li>Improved tracking of deadlines and required actions.</li>
          <li>Reduced risk of missed billing steps.</li>
          <li>Stronger foundation for operational compliance.</li>
        </ul>
      </article>
    </div>

    <div class="band">
      <h3>Project positioning</h3>
      <p>
        BOMS should be positioned as a workflow automation and operational control system. The value
        is in process standardization, reduction of manual workload, and improved visibility into billing
        operations rather than UI complexity or feature volume.
      </p>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>
